It's better to wing it


Love to travel but hate chasing your friends for input? No one wants to be 'that guy'. Especially not co-founder and mega-stressed-travel-planner, Tom Damant.
That's where Wing comes in.

Our simple set up will walk you through creating deadlines for key decisions, adding polls for gathering input and text reminders for those slow-to-respond friends.
No more bogeyman, no more last minute scrabble, no more stress. Just wing it.
